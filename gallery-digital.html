Digital Camera: Rishabh & Hyeseong

Description: This group's goal was to create a simple device using microcontrollers that allowed the user to record and take pictures. They accomplished this by using an ESP-32 module to take the pictures as well as a 3D printed design to house all of the circuit components. They also created a website where you can review the pictures taken by the camera live and also store all photos taken by the camera in an archive.
