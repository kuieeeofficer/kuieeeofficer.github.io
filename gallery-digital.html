About

Here at KU, our student branch aims to spark interest in the engineering profession and equip students with the skills and connections needed for successful careers. We regularly host industry professionals who share insights about their work and experiences, facilitate multiple team-driven projects centered around hardware and software science, and provide opportunities for our members to attend regional and national conferences.

EXPO Projects

The main thing our chapter focuses on is helping students design and build projects to present at the annual KU Engineering Expo. At the beginning of the year, project leads propose their ideas to the members and 5 projects our chosen through a voting process. For the next few months, project leads work with their team to bring their hardware or software project to life and present it at the Expo.

Officer Team

Currently, we have 7 officers on our board: President, Vice President, Secretary, Treasurer, Project Director, PR Chair, and Activities Chair. All of our officers are dedicated to growing KU IEEE and have contributed by leading projects or traveling to conferences.
